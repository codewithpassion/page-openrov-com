About OpenROV

THE SEARCH FOR THE UNKNOWN

OpenROV started out as a project among friends to get to the bottom of an underwater cave rumored to contain buried treasure. Since then OpenROV has grown into a company and community of people who are working together to create powerful new tools for underwater exploration.

CITIZEN SCIENCE INNOVATION

Our community has been a pioneering effort in the growing citizen science movement.
