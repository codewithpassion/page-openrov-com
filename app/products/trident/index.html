FAST & MANEUVERABLE

Trident can quickly scan large areas, but also maneuver precisely in close quarters.

With speeds up to 2m/s, up to 4h run time and a maximum depth of 100m, Trident sets new standards for this class of ROVs.

EASY TO USE

Use your phone or tablet to control the drone and stream the video.

You can stream full HD video straight to your device from deep underwater. Excellent color rendention, great low-light performance, and industry-leading latency make Trident the vehicle of choice for capturing quality video.

DURABLE & ULTRA PORTABLE

Trident's is compact enough to be carried in a backpack or tucked under an airplane seat and is tough enough to earn it’s keep in any professional setting.

AN EVOLUTIONARY LEAP IN CONNECTIVITY

Trident uses a wireless radio to communicate with the pilot’s phone, laptop, or tablet, which greatly increases Trident's effective range. A Kevlar-reinforced and neutrally-buoyant tether makes handling and retrieving Trident easy.
